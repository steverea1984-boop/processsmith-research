# OpenClaw Business Architecture Research

Research package for evaluating identity-aware OpenClaw deployments against enterprise agent/model platforms and SDK-based approaches for ProcessSmith.

## Source

- [Damian F. - Building an Identity-Aware AI Teammate for Real Work Using OpenClaw](source/damien-f-openclaw-identity-aware-ai-teammate.md)
- [Original DOCX capture](source/damien-f-openclaw-identity-aware-ai-teammate.docx)

## Working Questions

1. What is the safest practical architecture for deploying OpenClaw in a business setting?
2. Which responsibilities should belong to OpenClaw versus enterprise platforms such as Vertex AI, OpenAI, Azure, or AWS?
3. What identity, permission, credential, audit, and sandboxing controls are non-negotiable before ProcessSmith offers agentic business systems to clients?
4. Where is OpenClaw materially better than SDK-first or managed-enterprise setups, and where is it weaker?
5. What should ProcessSmith pilot first without overbuilding an enterprise platform too early?
